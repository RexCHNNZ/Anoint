SPARK-Intro

This page is a document for SPARK - A charity organization

What is SPARK?

SPARK is a non-profit organization built by students. We plan to promote students' original designs in schools and make these original designs into products for sale both on and off-campus. After deducting the cost, all the income from the sales will be donated to the people in need.

SPARK Purpose

Everyone can shine - Everyone can do charity and influence the world even though everyone is so little. We believe that everyone in the school can contribute to the charity and influence our community.

SPARK Future Plan

We are trying to create a connection with the school so that we can sponsor some house competitions or other interesting activities, with the principle of charity - we do not earn a profit!
